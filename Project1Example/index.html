Sharksidon

Scientific Name:

Selachimorphadion

Discovered by / at:

Michelle, February 20th, 2024

Family:

Sharksidon belongs to the class of Class Chondrichthyes, more specifically, an elasmobranchii. While they take the appearance of a regular shark, its large ego prevents itself from classifying itself as anything other than a god.

Habitat:

Sharksidon resides 2000 meters below sea level and love to graze among the coral reefs on the ocean floor.

Appearance:

Sharksidon is a gray shark that is approimately 18m long and holds a pointy trident.

Key Characteristics:

  • Summons lightning bolts when angry.
  • Enjoys terrorizing fish.
  • Blushes when fish are fed to him

What we know so far about Sharksidon? Sharksidon is a large aquatic mammal belonging to class Chondrichthyes. It is one of the tallest aquatic animals. Sharksidon is one of three brothers who wields magical powers. Unfortunately, anger issues run in his family bloodline and he usually takes his rage out of the fish around him. Sometimes, if he gets angry, he starts to flood villages on land though.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Sharksidon enjoys swimming on the ocean floor with little interuption, unless you prevent him with a fish! When gifting him a fish, he'll become extremely happy and flustered but will quickly become angry if you poke him; Thus resulting in lightning bolts spawning around him.
